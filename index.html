BioPieces
Biopieces is a talk show which mainly targets undergraduates and school leavers who want to explore biology and the opportunities it offers to future scientists and entrepreneurs.
Benzyme Reports on Medium
Benzyme report is an initiative to share latest research articles, research trends and innovations with the help of graduate, undergraduate students and postdocs around the world.
BenzymeNet
BenzymeNet is our social media forum which mainly focuses on career guidance of bioscience students and sharing opportunities available to undergraduates and postgraduates around the world.

Our Vision

To empower next-generation bioscience scientists and biotech entrepreneurs

Our Mission

To build a community of scientists, biotech leaders, bioscience students and biohackers to support and develop bioeconomy in developing countries in order to achieve sustainable development goals through cross border networking and collaborations.

To empower young leaders to start their own ventures in biotech and pharma through challenging projects, leadership training and project management.

Initiate research and development projects to achieve sustainable development goals and to battle climate change

Our Goals

1. Creating a scientific network for bioscience enthusiasts

2. Improving employability skills such as leadership, communication, team work and project management

3. Developing entrepreneurial mindsets of bioscience enthusiasts

4. Elevating the bioscience research output, innovation and creativity in the bioscience community
